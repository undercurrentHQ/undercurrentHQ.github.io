What is Undercurrent Games?

Undercurrent Games is currently a solo project by Guanpeng Chen, aiming to create games that have a flowing as well as lasting experience.

Now living in Kamakura Japan, Guanpeng works a full-time job and makes games whenever possible, with the hope of turning this project into a game studio.
